Milk Pail

Simple breastfeeding tracker

Keeping track of your babyâ€™s feeding schedule is important, but most apps overwhelm users with excessive options and fine grained details. These features can be useful for mothers who need to pump or use formula, but most mothers want to simply track session length and time so they can provide useful data for their pediatrician.

Milk Pail helps you do just that. Start your session by tapping Left or Right and then tap Start.

When you finish, tap End. Milk Pail automatically selects the alternate breast for your next session.

Information about all your sessions, including average session length (in total and per breast) is available in Stats.
